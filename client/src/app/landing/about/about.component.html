About Us

Who we are

EXCEL'ED was established in 2001 to support with the increasing demands placed on school finance staff. We specialise in the field of school financial management with an emphasis on both everyday working practices and strategic development.

Experience

Our experience dates back to the early days of Local Management of Schools and there has been much change since. In this ever expanding and demanding field we are at the forefront of the latest developments and constantly work to prepare schools to cope with change.
